2023: End of Streak

ustin Brownlee and Barangay Ginebra faced off against the TNT Tropang Giga in a thrilling six-game series. Brownlee started the series strong, showcasing his usual skill and determination, but faced an unexpected setback in Game 5. Just before the pivotal game, he suffered from food poisoning, which significantly impacted his health and performance. Despite this, Brownlee pushed through and attempted to play, but his energy and usual dominance were visibly affected.

TNT capitalized on Brownlee’s weakened state, managing to secure wins in Games 5 and 6 to clinch the championship, ultimately ending Ginebra's title defense. Though defeated, Brownlee’s effort and resilience despite his illness earned the respect of fans and players alike. His determination to compete, even under tough conditions, further cemented his legacy as one of Ginebra's most respected and beloved imports.
