2022: Slaying The Dragons

Justin Brownlee and Barangay Ginebra faced off against the Bay Area Dragons in an intense best-of-seven series. The finals were competitive, with both teams trading wins, but it was Game 7 that turned into a historic showdown. Brownlee once again rose to the occasion, showcasing his scoring ability, rebounding, and defensive skill. In front of a record-breaking crowd, he led Ginebra with crucial baskets, relentless hustle, and strong leadership, keeping the team focused and energized as they fought for every possession.

With the game on the line in the fourth quarter, Brownlee’s composure and timely plays allowed Ginebra to pull ahead and secure the victory. His efforts helped Ginebra claim the Commissioner’s Cup title, marking his sixth championship with the team. Brownlee’s Game 7 performance, highlighted by his versatility and clutch shooting, solidified his legacy as one of the most beloved imports in PBA history and a true icon for Ginebra fans.
